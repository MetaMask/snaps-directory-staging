Description by Wise Signer

Use AI to explain what your transactions are doing! This snap can identify address poisoning, malicious tokens, and other types of attacks! Of course, the AI can make mistakes, so be sure to do your own due diligence as well. How to use 1. Get a Claude API Key: console.anthropic.com 2. Configure the Snap: - Click on the Snap in MetaMask (Menu → Snaps → AI Transaction Explainer) - Paste your Claude API key and click 'Save API Key' - Choose your preferred model: -- Claude Opus 4: Most capable, best for complex transactions -- Claude Sonnet 4: Balanced performance and speed -- Claude Sonnet 3.7: Fastest responses - Toggle 'Auto-Explain' based on your preference And boom! Now the next transaction you make, you can have your MetaMask AI try to figure out what your transaction is doing. Watch this demo video for an in-depth explanation: youtu.be.
