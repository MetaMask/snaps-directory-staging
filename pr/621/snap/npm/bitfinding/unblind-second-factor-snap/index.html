Description by Unblind Second Factor

This Snap sends human-readable transaction summaries to your Telegram before you sign. It simulates each transaction and delivers a plain-English summary to your account. This lets you clearly see asset movements and contract interactions, helping you spot potential risks like wallet drainers or unexpected token transfers. How to Use: 1. Install the Snap and connect it to your Telegram account. 2. When you start a transaction, a summary will appear in your Telegram chat. 3. Review the details, then confirm the transaction in MetaMask. Security & Privacy: The Snap has read-only access to transaction data to generate explanations. It cannot access your private keys, sign transactions, or modify your assets.
